## Overview
This project analyzes socioeconomic and living conditions in European cities to help individuals identify locations that best balance income, cost of living, and employment opportunities.  
The work follows a complete data analysis workflow, from cleaning and exploring a real-world dataset to producing clear visual insights and recommendations.
## Objectives 
- Clean and preprocess a dataset of European cities
- Explore relationships between economic and social indicators
- Compare cities by affordability, employment, and overall quality of life
- Identify the best city for someone seeking high salary, low cost of living, and low unemployment
